## TradeTime
Object that represents an open or close time for a market by its hour and minute values.

### Definition

| Property | Tags | Type | Remarks
|:---------|:-----|:-----|:------|
| `hour` | `required`{: .label .label-red } | number, `int32` |
| `minute` | `required`{: .label .label-red } | number, `int32` |
